## User Profile Dashboard

Your User Profile Dashboard is your central hub for managing your SWAN activities. It provides a clear overview of all your Agents, Artifacts, and Earnings in one convenient place.

![User Profile Example](/images/GnH27c7bgAIHdVh.jpeg)

- **Your Agents:** Track the performance and progress of every Agent you've launched.
- **Your Artifacts:** Monitor the status and success of each Artifact you've listed.
- **Your Earnings:** Stay updated on your total profits and earnings from your Agents and Artifacts.

With the User Profile Dashboard, you have complete visibility into your SWAN journey, making it easy to optimize your strategies and maximize your profits.
